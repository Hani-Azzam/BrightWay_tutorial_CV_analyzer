JOB TITLE:   Senior AI Backend Engineer
COMPANY:     Nexora AI
LOCATION:    Tel Aviv, Israel (Hybrid — 3 days on-site)
TYPE:        Full-time

------------------------------------------------------------------------
ABOUT THE ROLE
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Nexora AI is building the next generation of enterprise knowledge assistants.
We are looking for a Senior AI Backend Engineer to design, build, and
operate production-grade RAG pipelines and LLM-powered services. You will
work closely with our ML researchers, product managers, and data engineers
to ship features that reach thousands of enterprise users.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS
------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. At least 4 years of professional backend software engineering experience,
   with Python as the primary language.

2. Hands-on production experience with RAG systems — including chunking
   strategies, embedding models, vector databases (Pinecone, Weaviate,
   or equivalent), and retrieval evaluation.

3. Solid understanding of LLM integration patterns: prompt engineering,
   tool use, memory management, and responsible AI practices
   (PII handling, audit logging, hallucination mitigation).

4. Experience designing and maintaining REST APIs at scale using
   frameworks such as FastAPI or Flask, with attention to latency,
   reliability, and observability.

5. Proficiency with cloud infrastructure (AWS or GCP), containerisation
   (Docker), and CI/CD pipelines; comfortable owning services in production.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
NICE-TO-HAVE QUALIFICATIONS
------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Experience with ML model fine-tuning (LoRA, PEFT) or working directly
   with embedding model customisation.

2. Open-source contributions or personal projects that demonstrate
   initiative in the AI/LLM space.

3. Familiarity with agentic frameworks such as LangChain, LangGraph,
   or AutoGen, and experience building multi-step agent workflows.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT WE OFFER
------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Competitive salary: 35,000 – 50,000 NIS / month depending on experience
- Equity package
- Hybrid work model with a modern Tel Aviv office
- Personal learning budget of 8,000 NIS / year
- Direct impact on a product used by Fortune 500 companies
